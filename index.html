birdbirdclose

Introducing birdbird, a full-stack app designed for the casual bird watcher. birdbird lets you record a bird sighting with ease and see a map of all bird sightings in the San Francisco area. You can also track your sightings and species observed in your notebook. birdbird is based on a similar app produced by the Cornell Lab of Ornithology, but adapted to be brighter, simpler, and with a more logical user flow / navigation.
It utilizes the Google Maps API for viewing and recording bird sighting locations. Additionally, the project emphasized gaining familiarity with the model-view-controller architecture of Django and uses Postgres for the database. For the front-end, I used HTML, CSS, Javascript, and jQuery.
